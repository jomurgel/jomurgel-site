Hardcore Comms exists to make radio communication approachable, practical, and honest — to cut through the noise, the intimidation, and the "tactical" hype around radios, licensing, and getting on the air. Good mission. Hard to act on when nobody can find you.

Because going in, there was no website at all. No presence, no home base, nothing to point people toward.

So this one started from zero, which I prefer. A full design and build on WordPress, set up less as a brochure and more as a foundation — the brief wasn't "a site," it was a place that could grow into training, ecommerce, and whatever expansion comes next without needing to be torn down to get there.
