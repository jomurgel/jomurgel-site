The site they had didn't carry any of that. It was expensive to run, a pain to maintain, and it didn't look like a company with plans to grow — which they very much are.

So I took it end to end: a full redesign and a rebuild on WordPress. The platform choice was the point. Moving them off something costly and fragile onto something the team can edit themselves, extend as they add programs, and not dread the bill for — that's the part that actually changes their year. The design does the other half of the job and makes the site look like the operation they're running.

Design and execution were mine, start to finish.
